Chapter One
Roots in Hoshiarpur

"Every great story starts somewhere — mine began in the warm, bustling lanes of Hoshiarpur, Punjab."

Born on 21st October 1990 in Hoshiarpur, India, I grew up speaking Punjabi, surrounded by community, hard work, and a deep desire to build something meaningful with my hands and my presence.

My formal education took me through the Punjab School Education Board, where I completed my Higher Secondary Education in 2009 — laying the foundation for a life of discipline and continuous learning.

Chapter Two
Building with My Hands

My professional story began not in an office but on the open ground — as a Site Supervisor at R.K. Aggarwal & Co., overseeing road construction across India from 2012 to 2016. It was demanding, physical, and deeply formative.

🏗️
Site Supervisor
R.K. Aggarwal & Co. · Apr 2012 – Sep 2016
Led labor teams, coordinated materials, monitored daily construction activities, and reported progress to management. Four years of grit.
📒
Book-keeper
R.K. Aggarwal & Co. · Oct 2016 – Jul 2018
Shifted from site to office — managing bookkeeping, payroll, invoices, and daily accounting. A new chapter of precision and detail.

After four years on construction sites, I transitioned into the world of numbers and records — becoming a Book-keeper at the same firm. It was a pivot that taught me adaptability, the same quality that would later define my career.

Chapter Three
A New World: Malaysia

"In 2018, I crossed an ocean — and discovered a version of myself I hadn't met before."

In August 2018, I made one of the boldest decisions of my life — leaving India for Selangor, Malaysia, to join Kopitiam 80 as a Head Waiter. What began as a new job became a seven-year chapter of growth, multicultural connection, and professional excellence.

☕
Head Waiter
Kopitiam 80 · Aug 2018 – Dec 2025
Led service excellence in a vibrant multicultural setting. Every day was about people — their comfort, their orders, their smiles.
🌏
Multicultural Life
Selangor, Malaysia
Worked alongside colleagues from diverse backgrounds, learning Malay, navigating new customs, and growing as a person.
💳
Operations
POS · Billing · Maintenance
Beyond service — managed billing systems, cash handling, staff shifts, and facility maintenance with steady hands.

Seven years. Thousands of customers. Countless moments of service. Malaysia shaped me into a professional who thrives under pressure, communicates across cultures, and finds dignity in every role.
